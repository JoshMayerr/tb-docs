# Manage your Organization

If you are an admin for your organization, you will be able to invite other members to the Tollbit dashboard. To get started,
navigate to the [Settings](https://app.tollbit.com/settings) page.

## Invite Members {{ anchor: false }}

You can invite new members to your organization and assign them roles when they join.
![](/invite-members.png)

## Manage Members {{ anchor: false }}

You can manage your existing members, change their roles, or remove them for your organization.
![](/manage-members.png)
